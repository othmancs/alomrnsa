Website Proof of Payment Attachment

With Odoo payment proof attachment feature the customers can attach the payment receipt with their website orders in case they made payment through wire transfer or any other manual mode. You can see the attachment in the respective order in the backend.

What Is Odoo Proof Of Payment Attachment?

  • Proof of attachment is the receipt that is generated during the wire transfer.
  • The receipt can be in png or pdf format.
  • The payment receipt is attached after placing the order on the website by the customer for the Odoo admin.

Why Do You Need It ?

  • Start processing order or service requests immediately.
  • To ease Admin-buyer communication on orders placed via wire transfer
  • Reduce delay in services to customers.

Detailed feature list for Odoo Proof of Payment

  • Facilitates the customers to add proof of payment for Odoo website orders.
  • Odoo proof of payment can be a pdf or png format file.
  • The option becomes available for all the manual transfer payment methods enabled on the website.
  • Alternatively he can go to 'My account' and attach the proof of payment in the orders section.
  • The admin can view the Odoo payment proof attachment in customer's order in the backend.
