Overview

  • By using this module, you can add dynamic terms & conditions to the report.

Features

You can set dynamic terms & conditions in the sale, purchase, and invoice report.

Screenshots

Set configuration terms & conditions either country or company wise.

If the terms & conditions are for a specific country, you can set them according to country wise.

If the terms & conditions are for a specific company, you can set them according to company wise.

If you want to apply the same terms & conditions to Sale, Purchase, and Invoice, click here.

The terms & conditions set by you will be showcased in your sales, invoice, and purchase report.
